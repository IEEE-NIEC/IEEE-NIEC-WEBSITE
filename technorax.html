TECHNORAX V2.0

The IEEE NIEC organized TECHNORAX 2.0- The Technical Exhibition on 4th Oct 2016 at Northern India Engineering College (NIEC), as a descendant to the previous year’s well acclaimed event Technorax. Held as a part of the worldwide IEEE Day celebrations, the event was aimed to create a technical environment amongst the ensuing engineers so as to provide them the right platform to bring out their talent and discover a plethora of opportunities. With more than 200 participants from various colleges who displayed their innovative projects and judged by professionals from the industry, the event turned out to be a great success despite of rainfall which couldn’t ruin the ambience and indomitable spirit of the organizing team and participants. As a special attraction a Robo-Race was conducted in collaboration with M2R Technomations where 25+ zealous teams competed with their bots. The project awarded first price from judges was "Signal Classification using Bayesian and Levenberg and HRV(heart rate variablity)" made by Munish Suri from AIACTR, GGSIPU. First runner up was project named "UAV Photogrammetry" made by Harshit Batra, Abhishek Jain, Nikhil v Kumar, Pratyaksh, Chetan, Vibhav, Munish and lovneet from Northern India Engineering College, New Delhi. The Second runner up was project name "electronic guitar" made by Aman Gupta and Soham Roy. As a valediction, certificates and prizes were distributed to the winners as well as the participants.
